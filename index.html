Things to Do

Wine Tasting

Paso Robles is known for having some of the best wineries in the central coast. There are many great options, below are a few we have visited in the past and enjoyed

  • Castoro Cellars: our go-to for enjoying a glass in their outdoor patio
  • Eberle Winery: tours available of their wine cave and free tastings

Breweries

One of the major west coast breweries, Firestone Walker, offers tasting, tours, and food at their flagship Paso Robles location.

Downtown Paso

Several shops and restaurants in this quaint downtown square. Some of our favorite include:

  • Brown butter cookie company: Veronica's favorite cookies and a must stop anytime we visit
  • Eleven Twenty Two: speakeasy with large cocktail selection

Travel

Getting to Paso

: Paso Robles is located in the central coast of California. The nearest major airport is in San Luis Obispo (SLO) which is about an hour drive from the airport.

: Alternatively, if you choose to drive from Los Angeles it is about 3-4 hours north of the city.
